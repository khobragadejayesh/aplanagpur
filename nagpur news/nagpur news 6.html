CR Nagpur Division Mega Ticket checking Drive fetches Rs 13.02 Lakhs in one day

NUnder the guidance of Smt Richa Khare, Divisional Railway Manager and monitoring of Shri Krishnath Patil Senior Divisional Commercial Manager, a Mega-Ticket checking drive was conducted over the Nagpur Division of the Central Railway on 13.9.2022, at Nagpur, Wardha and Ballarshah During the drive Rs 13.02 Lakhs from 2128 passengers were realized from without ticket/ Irregular traveling passengers and towards Unbooked luggage carried by the passengers. The check was supervised by Shri Sanjay Muley, Assistant Commercial Manager (Ticket Checking).

The Nagpur Division is making all out efforts to ensure a comfortable and convenient travel to its passengers. Railways fervently appeals to the travelling public to travel with valid Journey tickets.
